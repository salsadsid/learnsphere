# Day 57 Learning Notes

## Why this implementation is needed
- Instructors need visibility into learner momentum.
- CSV exports enable offline reporting.

## Advantages
- Immediate insight into completion rates.
- Easy sharing with stakeholders.

## Disadvantages
- Reports are only as good as available progress data.

## Alternatives and tradeoffs
- Embed analytics charts inside the dashboard.
- Export to a reporting service instead of CSV.

## Concepts and terms
- Progress summary: per-student view of completion.
- CSV export: lightweight data sharing format.

## Fundamentals
- Keep instructor views permissioned.
- Present summaries before deep analytics.
