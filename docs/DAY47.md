# Day 47 Learning Notes

## Why this implementation is needed
- Learners need an in-app video player.
- Playback controls are foundational UX.

## Advantages
- Uses native browser controls.
- Simple to extend later.

## Disadvantages
- Limited styling without custom controls.

## Alternatives and tradeoffs
- Use a custom player library.
- Build bespoke controls for branding.

## Concepts and terms
- Playback controls: play, pause, seek, volume.

## Fundamentals
- Keep controls accessible and consistent.
- Optimize for mobile playback.
