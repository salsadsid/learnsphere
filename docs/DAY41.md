# Day 41 Learning Notes

## Why this implementation is needed
- Instructors need a clear view of their course portfolio.
- Summary stats provide quick health signals.
- Dashboards reduce context switching.

## Advantages
- Centralized instructor overview.
- Quick access to draft vs published status.
- Encourages steady content iteration.

## Disadvantages
- In-memory counts reset on restart.
- No historical trends yet.
- Limited to instructor role only.

## Alternatives and tradeoffs
- Use a dedicated instructor area separate from dashboard.
- Add analytics later to avoid early complexity.

## Concepts and terms
- Instructor dashboard: curated view of owned courses.
- Summary stats: counts that highlight status distribution.

## Fundamentals
- Keep metrics simple and actionable.
- Align list data with edit workflows.
