# Day 54 Learning Notes

## Why this implementation is needed
- Learners want a sense of overall course progress.

## Advantages
- Quick summary of completion.
- Motivates continued learning.

## Disadvantages
- Requires keeping completion counts in sync.

## Alternatives and tradeoffs
- Calculate progress client-side only.
- Use weighted completion based on lesson length.

## Concepts and terms
- Aggregation: summarizing lesson data at the course level.

## Fundamentals
- Keep progress math transparent.
- Avoid hidden weighting unless needed.
