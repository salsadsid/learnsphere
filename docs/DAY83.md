# Day 83 Learning Notes

## Why this implementation is needed
- Instructors need signals beyond completion to judge course health.

## Advantages
- Engagement metrics highlight where learners stall.
- Active learner counts show cohort momentum.

## Disadvantages
- Metrics can lag without frequent snapshots.
- Requires careful interpretation with small cohorts.

## Alternatives and tradeoffs
- Use surveys for engagement (qualitative but slower).
- Track only completions (simpler, less insight).

## Concepts and terms
- Engagement metrics: composite measures of activity.
- Active learners: users with recent activity.

## Fundamentals
- Combine multiple signals for more reliable course performance insights.
