# Day 71 Learning Notes

## Why this implementation is needed
- Users need visibility into billing history.

## Advantages
- Quick access to payment status and timing.

## Disadvantages
- History is in-memory only for now.

## Alternatives and tradeoffs
- Persist history in a payments database.

## Concepts and terms
- Invoice history: record of payment attempts.

## Fundamentals
- Provide clear status and timestamps.
