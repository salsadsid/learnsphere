# Day 66 Learning Notes

## Why this implementation is needed
- Learners need transparency about payment state.

## Advantages
- Clear status reporting for pending or failed payments.

## Disadvantages
- Requires an additional status request.

## Alternatives and tradeoffs
- Include payment status in enrollment status endpoint.

## Concepts and terms
- Payment status: pending/paid/failed state.

## Fundamentals
- Surface status in UI for clarity.
