# Day 78 Learning Notes

## Why this implementation is needed
- Teams need visibility into job health.

## Advantages
- Simple metrics show queue pressure.

## Disadvantages
- Logs can be noisy without sampling.

## Alternatives and tradeoffs
- Use a metrics system (Prometheus) later.

## Concepts and terms
- Queue metrics: counts of job outcomes.

## Fundamentals
- Log the lifecycle of background jobs.
